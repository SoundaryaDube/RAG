# Stage 2 (Detailed submission) – max 10 slides deck outline

## Slide 1 — Product story
Unified Student Engagement Ecosystem: engage students early, build trust, convert to loan applications.

## Slide 2 — Problem + stakes
Fragmented journey; drop-offs due to uncertainty, deadlines, and financing confusion.

## Slide 3 — Persona + lifecycle
Persona + 5-stage journey; define “North Star action” per stage.

## Slide 4 — Prototype walkthrough (screens)
Career Navigator → ROI planner → Admit score → Timeline → Mentor Copilot → Loan Funnel.

## Slide 5 — Personalization & growth loops
Stage-aware nudges, streaks, referrals, AI content engine, smart notifications.

## Slide 6 — Conversion layer
Eligibility estimator, EMI scenarios, doc checklist completion, AI-assisted application draft.

## Slide 7 — AI architecture
LLM for mentor + drafting; scoring models; optional RAG with approved content; guardrails.

## Slide 8 — Data, safety, and compliance
PII handling, prompt injection protection, audit logs, answer grounding, model monitoring.

## Slide 9 — Metrics + expected impact
Activation, retention, conversion; time saved; reduced drop-offs; improved application readiness.

## Slide 10 — Roadmap
Integrations (CRM/loan origination), real underwriting signals, agentic automation, multilingual support.

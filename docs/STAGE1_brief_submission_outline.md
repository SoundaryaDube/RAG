# Stage 1 (Brief submission) – slide-ready outline (8–10 slides)

## Slide 1 — Title + one-liner
**Unified Student Engagement Ecosystem (AI-first)**  
An AI-first student “SoFi-like” platform that turns study-abroad exploration into **loan-ready** intent via personalized engagement loops.

## Slide 2 — Problem
- Student journey is fragmented: discovery → admissions → visa → financial planning → loan application.
- Information overload + lack of trust creates drop-offs.
- Financing discovery happens too late → low conversion.

## Slide 3 — Persona + journey stages
**Target:** India-based UG final year / young professionals  
**Stages:** Exploration → Shortlisting → Applications → Admit → Visa/Onboarding  
Key jobs-to-be-done: “choose program”, “estimate ROI”, “hit deadlines”, “prepare documents”, “fund education”.

## Slide 4 — Solution (platform modules)
- Career Navigator (countries/unis/courses)
- Admission Probability + improvement tips
- Application Timeline Generator
- ROI + cost planning
- Conversational Mentor Copilot (LLM)
- Loan Funnel: eligibility → EMI → checklist → AI-assisted application draft

## Slide 5 — AI architecture (what uses what)
- **LLM**: mentor chat, SOP/essay drafting, next-best-action explanations
- **Predictive/Scoring models**: admit score, eligibility estimator, program fit ranking
- **Personalization**: stage-aware nudges, dynamic content feed
- (Optional) embeddings/vector DB: retrieval grounded in approved content

## Slide 6 — Growth engine (acquire + make sticky)
- AI-generated content: reels/blogs/newsletters per stage (SEO + social)
- Viral loops: referral rewards, streaks, challenges (“7-day application sprint”)
- Smart nudges: WhatsApp/push/email based on stage + inactivity
- Community hooks: cohort groups, peer Q&A, mentor sessions

## Slide 7 — Conversion thinking (NBFC lens)
- Reduce friction: precheck eligibility early + document readiness score
- Increase trust: transparent calculators, citations, “why this offer”
- Drop-off prevention: staged application, autofill draft, reminders
- Compliance: PII-safe, audit logs, policy-based answers

## Slide 8 — Monetization + business impact
- Primary: education loan acquisition (high-intent funnel)
- Secondary: partner lead gen (tests/coaching), premium mentor features
- KPIs: CAC, activation, DAU/MAU, completion of checklist, application submit rate

## Slide 9 — Execution plan to prototype (03/05/26)
- Week 1: UX + core modules + dataset + scoring models
- Week 2: mentor chat + personalization + loan funnel flow
- Week 3: polish demo, evaluation, deployment, deck + video

## Slide 10 — Why we win (judging criteria)
- “Wow” sticky experience + end-to-end funnel
- Deep AI usage (LLM + scoring + personalization loops)
- Clear conversion path → loan application readiness
